**Synthesis
In a <strong><em>syntheis</em></strong> reaction, two or more reactants combine to form a <strong>SINGLE product</strong>.
For example: 4Fe + 3O<sub>2</sub> -> 2Fe<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub>
The reactants may be elements, compounds, or a mix of elements and compounds.
Here's a synthesis reaction involving compounds:<br> CaO + 2CO<sub>2</sub> + H<sub>2</sub>O -> Ca(HCO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub>
Synthesis reactions are also called <em>combination</em> reactions.
